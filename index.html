About Us

Borsch Club is a University of California, Berkeley student organization which unites students interested in exploring and connecting with the cultures of the countries in Eastern Europe and Central Asia. We are a student-run organization which aims to form a community interested in expanding their knowledge of histories, cultures, and languages by participating in cultural, social, and academic events and activities.
